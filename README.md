# Royal-Run

Dodge, dash, and race against time in **Royal Run**, an infinite runner where every second counts.  
Procedurally generated obstacles keep each run unique, while speed boosts, traps, and flying hazards test your reflexes. Collect coins, extend your timer by passing checkpoints, and see how far you can go before the clock runs out.  

---

*Features
- **Infinite runner gameplay** with procedurally generated obstacles.  
- **Speed boost power-up** to cover more ground quickly.  
- **Flying objects** that slow you down when collided with.  
- **Fence obstacles** that block your path.  
- **Timer-based challenge** , the level ends if time runs out.  
- **Checkpoints** that reward extra time.  
- **Collectible coins** that increase your score.  

---

*How to Play
1. Control your runner and dodge procedurally generated obstacles.  
2. Collect **coins** to increase your score.  
3. Pass **checkpoints** to gain extra time.  
4. Avoid fences and flying objects that can slow you down.  
5. Use **speed boosts** wisely to cover more ground.  
6. Keep running until the timer hits zero or until you crash!
